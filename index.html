Dalgona Coffee Recipe

Dalgona Coffee has a been a popullar beverage during the lockdown and has become a trend on social media. The South Korean drink has become a frequent lockdown activity. You will likely have all of the ingredients required in your home already which will make it easier to make. This means that everyone has to try drinking it!

Ingredients

  • Instant Coffee Powder or Espresso Granules
  • Hot Water
  • Sugar
  • Milk

Dalgona Making Process

Step 1: Add coffee powder to hot water
Add 2 tablespoons of coffee powder 3 tablespoons of sugar to 1 cup of hot water. The water must be very hot.

Step 2: Whisk the mixture untik turns fluffy
Using hand held mixer, whisk the coffee mixture till the mixture turns into a light brown color with creaminess.

Step 3: Serve it with hot or cold milk
Serve the coffee cream with hot or cold milk, a straw, and a spoons. Then....Enjoy!
